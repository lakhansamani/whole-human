Whole Human is a lifestyle brand, a physical campus, and a one-of-a kind membership experience. Most importantly, it’s a fun-loving community working towards a more meaningful normal, while focusing on balance between social connection, professional life, and personal wellness.

Mission

To curate one's Whole Human potential alongside an inspiring, loving, real community dedicated to a new normal - one of interconnection - bridging a harmony between social connection, professional creation, and personal wellness.

Vision

Bridging the worlds of Nosara (between expats and locals, nature and technology, self and community, action and recovery) by being the go-to hub for connection, creation, and inspiration. Your daily retreat.

Mental Wellbeing

  • Silent Meditation Room: A peaceful indoor space for meditation or prayer, for solitude, mindfulness and reflection.
  • Specialized Healing Treatments: Options for unique therapies located in “Healing Huts” such as acupuncture, reiki, somatic therapy, and dry needling.
  • Sensory Deprivation Tanks: Self-use tanks in a designated recovery room for deep relaxation and mental reset.
  • Outdoor “resting zone”: Dedicated quiet zones with mats, pods and hammocks to reflect, nap/NSDR, or simply take mental space from stimulation.
  • Facility Based Frequencies: Different sounds/frequencies in each area of the facility to support nervous system regulation.

Physical Wellbeing

  • Creative Movement Tools: Unique equipment such as slacklines, surf trainers, monkey bars, and rings for functional, fun training.
  • Recovery Zone: Dedicated space with self-use recovery tools like foam rollers, Theraguns, Ice packs and compression boots.
  • Silent Meditation Room: A peaceful indoor space for meditation or prayer, for solitude, mindfulness and reflection.
  • Bio-hack Zone: Equipped with all the modern tech such as full body Infrared panels, localized cryotherapy, hyperbaric chamber, NeuroVizrs (light & sound technology) and TENS units.
  • Hot and Cold Plunges: A mix of communal and private plunge pools for contrast therapy, paired with an infrared sauna for deep recovery.
  • Outdoor Functional Movement Space: Large open-air gym area with CrossFit-style equipment like sleds, tires, and pull-up bars.
  • Custom Juice and Smoothie Bar: Create-your-own smoothies and juices using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, available with added supplements and nootropics.

Communal Wellbeing

  • Community Courtyard: A vibrant social space near the pool and café, designed to foster community interaction and connection.
  • Canopy Lounge Lofts: Elevated seating or lounging areas in the trees, perfect for quiet conversations or socializing in nature.
  • Outdoor Social Seating Areas: Comfortable lounging spots overlooking the pool or surrounded by nature, ideal for informal gatherings.
  • Themed Social Events: A courtyard space for hosting weekly community events, workshops, movie nights, and mixers.
  • Collaborative Dining Space: Outdoor café seating designed for social meals, providing a relaxing atmosphere near the pool.
  • Community Garden: Dedicated outdoor space for communal gardening

Recreational Wellbeing

  • Natural Pool: A UV-sanitized pool with comfortable loungers, water features, and daybeds for a relaxing swim and socializing.
  • Creative Play Equipment: Slackline, surf trainers, balance beams, and monkey bars for active play and movement exploration.
  • Unique Event Shala Features: Floor-to-ceiling glass windows that open up to the surrounding jungle, providing the option to be indoors or open air for an immersive nature experience during yoga or movement classes.
  • Outdoor Jungle Gym: A fun, functional fitness space with CrossFit-style equipment such as sleds, rings, and pull-up bars.
  • Climbing Wall: A versatile outdoor climbing wall, offering a fun challenge for all skill levels, encouraging adventure and movement exploration.
  • Quotes/Invitations: Inspiring quotes/reminders, weekly invitations and resources  around the land and in the facilities

Work Wellbeing

  • Creative Workstations: Includes treadmill desks, floor (grounding) seating and lap tables for flexible, dynamic work setups.
  • Podcast Booth: A professional-grade recording booth with sound and light engineering for podcasts, webinars, or virtual meetings.
  • Private Soundproof Pods: Quiet, enclosed spaces for phone calls, focused work, or virtual meetings, offering complete privacy.
  • Collaborative Meeting Spaces: Flexible meeting spaces with large tables, whiteboards, and monitors for team collaborations and group work.
  • Unique “Seating”: Ergonomic work space options like exercise balls, standing desks, bean bags and balancing boards to encourage movement and dynamic posture while working.

Environmental Wellbeing

  • Grounding Zone: A dedicated tech-free space with grass, stone-stacking areas, and walking paths, encouraging grounding and connection with the earth.
  • Living Plant Walls: Indoor vertical gardens or plant walls that promote biophilia, enhancing the connection to nature inside the facility.
  • Outdoor Showers: Nature-inspired showers with greenery and water features, offering a refreshing and eco-friendly post-workout experience.
  • Waste/Compost Recycling Systems: Sustainable waste management through composting and recycling, encouraging eco-conscious habits.
  • Water Features and Landscaping: Tranquil water features and lush landscaping integrated throughout the facility to create a serene and natural environment.
  • Horticulture Classes: Utilizing the land and community garden to learn how to grow and harvest your own food.
